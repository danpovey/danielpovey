Job postings (most recent first)

This page is a list of available jobs that I have heard about, in speech recognition and related fields. I am maintaining this page because I frequently get told about available jobs, and often don't have a good idea where to forward the information.

These are mostly postings from smaller companies. The market for speech recognition talent is very tight at the moment, and you can assume that for qualified candidates, the big players such as Google, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, IBM and BBN, and of course Nuance, always have open positions.
